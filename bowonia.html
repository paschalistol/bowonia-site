The story

You are the last surviving Bowonian. A former citizen of the mighty city state of Bowonia. And the last remnants of the ancient guard has now fallen. Before the crisis, you were but a mere recruit. Now the fate of the world is in your hands. But how could you defeat an evil so great that not even the combined will- and might of your people could? Endless hordes of twisted UwUs will rush towards you, as they rip at your very flesh. Do they deserve mercy? NO! These creatures may look like you, but they are not of your kin. Treat them as they treated your people. Show them how it feels to be on the receiving end of Bowonian fury.

Marco Powo

Marco Powo, of the species OwO is the protagonist of the game

Fighter

Fighters are skilled warriors that excel in melee! They employ a variety of offensive and defensive skills to trample over their enemies while absorbing damage through shields and healing strikes. They use rage to fuel their abilities, which is gained by attacking the enemy. Rage will decay after a short time. Rage may also be gained through consuming potions form the store or from pickups on the map.

Mystic

Mystics are artists of magic. They control the battlefield from afar with devestating ranged spells while also outmaneuvering their enemies with freezing blasts and teleports. The mystic use mana, a finite resource that fuel all of their spells. Mana starts out full. Mana can be regained by using mana potions or through pickups on the map.

The UwUs

There are 3 different kinds of UwUs, the Millenial, the Zoomer and the Boomer.

Millenial

The Millenial is an UwU, driven by a mad conviction to kill every single OwO in existence for the glory of all UwUs. The basic enemy type with average health and speed. Will cowardly backup a little if close to the player and the player gets a multiple kills quickly.

Zoomer

A Zoomer is a variant on the simple fanatic. They’ve embraced their inner animal, gaining horns and increased speed while losing some body mass. They have more speed than fanatics but less health.

Boomer

The Boomer is an UwU who bottled all of its hatred for OwOs inside its body, growing in size and strength. It has a lot of health and slow speed. Its hatred is so pure, it has become explosive. Upon death after a short delay, its feelings burst out in a deadly explosion.

The Enchanter

The enchanter is an old but nice UwU that wants to help the player. He sells potions and new skills and also upgrades them.

Crystals

To buy from the enchanter he'll need something in exchange, and that currency is crystals. The Bowonian crystals are very valuable and very rare to find. Luckily for you, every UwU carries some. Kill all the UwU to upgrade your skills because they get stronger and stronger.

Potions

There are 3 kind of spells but each class can use only 2 of them.

Health Potion
The health potion is common for both classes and restores a significant amount of health when consumed
Rage potion
A rage potion that increases Fighter's rage when consumed.
Mana Potion
A mana potion that restores Mystic's mana when consumed

Offensive

Mystic - Launch an arcane bolt from your hands, obliterating enemies with arcane vigour!
Meteor (Mystic)
Call down a devastating meteor, dealing damage in an area! Has a small cast time.
Chain Lightning (Mystic)
Shoot a lightning bolt that chains to enemies!
Groundstomp (Fighter)
Stomp the ground, dealing damage and stunning enemies around you!

Defensive

Berserk (Fighter)
Enter a frenzy, gaining full rage, defense and attack speed bonuses!
Ice Nova (Mystic)
'Unleash a freezing nova around you, preventing enemies from moving.'
Juggernaut (Fighter)
Become impervious to damage for some seconds!

Utilities

Blink (Mystic)
Teleport a small distance through space and time, gaining a movement speed bost!
Charge (Fighter)
Launch yourself forward, dashing your way through enemy lines. Dealing damage to everyone in your path.
Life leech (Fighter)
Steal the life out of your enemies! Next 5 basic attacks heals you over time!

Objectives

Kill the boss

A strong enemy will hunt you! Kill it before it leaves the area to win the award.

Collect the objects

Some emergency supplies will arrive in Bowonia but they are scattered around the map! Gather them before the wave is over to receive a reward!

Protect the building

The UwUs will target one of your buildings. Defend it to save your city.
