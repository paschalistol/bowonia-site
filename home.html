Welcome to the world of Bowonia.

Bowonia is a survival fast-paced RPG with cartoony style made with Unity.

Watch the trailer

The story

You are the last surviving Bowonian. A former citizen of the mighty city state of Bowonia. And the last remnants of the ancient guard has now fallen. Before the crisis, you were but a mere recruit. Now the fate of the world is in your hands. But how could you defeat an evil so great that not even the combined will- and might of your people could? Endless hordes of twisted UwUs will rush towards you, as they rip at your very flesh. Do they deserve mercy? NO! These creatures may look like you, but they are not of your kin. Treat them as they treated your people. Show them how it feels to be on the receiving end of Bowonian fury.

Survive as long as you can

  • Fight back against endless waves of enemies as they try to bring you down
  • Seek out the enchanter in search of new powers
  • Learn to navigate the chaotic streets of Bowonia; make it your personal battlefields

Choose your destiny

  • Play as the Fighter or Mystic; two classes with different strengths and weaknesses
  • Customize your playstyle with different abilities
  • Participate in events around the map

An experience for everyone

  • Play the way you like with fully remappable controls
  • Function customization for increased availability
