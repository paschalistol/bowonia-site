Group 3 Studios

Bowonia was created as a school project by 6 students of the Stockholm University's Game Development program. When asigned into groups, the team got the number 3, which they decided to keep as an official team name and therefore the game is made from Group 3 Studios The development started in October of 2019 and finished in early January of the following year when the game was part of an exhibition at the Swedish National Museum of Science and Technology.

Team Members
Name Role Description
Alexander Producer - Engineer Alex was the producer of the studio with main responsibility that all members work good together and there are no problems that hold the project back. He was also one of the 3 engineers and worked mainly with the character skills.
Emil Engineer - Writer Emil was one of the 3 engineers of Group 3 studios with main focus on the enemy skills and intelligence. He was also the writer and historian of the group writing all texts.
Johan 3D artist - Environment Builder Johan was the architect of the team, creating a beautiful new planet with different areas and a huge variety of buildings and props.
Leo Lead Artist - Character Designer Leo is responsible for all the great characters on Bowonia. He designed them and them animated both the player and all the enemies.
Paschalis Lead Engineer - UX Designer Paschalis was the last of the 3 engineers of Group 3 Studios. He didn't have a scecific area of responsibility, instead he had oversight of all code and optimized it. He was also the UX designer of the team.
Theo Lead Designer - Management Theo is the mastermind behind Bowonia. He designed every skill and behaviour in the game and came with all the creative ideas. He took also over all the bureaucracy and management.
All the members did more than what was described, such as trailer editing, quest developing, marketing etc.
